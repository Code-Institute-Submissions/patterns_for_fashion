About Patterns 4 Fashion

Our team is formed by skilled professional pattern-makers and seamstresses specialised in creating dresses and shirts on different sizes and body proportions. For creating the patterns, our professionals are using a very methodical technique named "Danckaerts". ... Dankckaerts technique is a very old and well thought system for creating clothing patterns and was developed in 1890 by the Danckaerts family: Mrs Danckaerts, tailor and her husband Mr Danckaerts, engineer. Together they built the Danckaerts Fashion School which grew constantly with every new generation of students and teachers. Their notable success was due to the very accurate formulas and methodology they developed while studying the human body proportions which can be found in so many sizes and shapes.
